Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Nagachethana PU College.

What streams are offered at Nagachethana PU College?

Nagachethana PU College offers two streams: Science (PCMB – Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology; and PCMC – Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Computer Science) and Commerce (CEBA and HEBA combinations). Integrated NEET, KCET, and JEE coaching is included for Science students.

What is the pass rate at Nagachethana PU College?

Nagachethana PU College consistently achieves a 98% pass rate in Karnataka PUC board examinations. Since its founding in 2015, the college has produced 50+ toppers and more than 2,000 graduates across Science and Commerce streams.

How do I apply for admission in 2026-27?

To apply for admission at Nagachethana PU College for 2026-27, visit the college at Yelahanka or fill the enquiry form on this website. Required documents: 10th mark sheet, transfer certificate, and passport-size photos. Call +91 97390 85747 for immediate assistance.

Does the college offer NEET and CET coaching?

Yes. Nagachethana PU College offers integrated NEET, KCET, and JEE coaching alongside the regular PUC Science curriculum. Students receive dedicated entrance exam preparation from experienced faculty without needing to attend separate coaching centres.

What is the college code of Nagachethana PU College?

The college code of Nagachethana PU College is AN0928, recognised by the Department of Pre-University Education, Government of Karnataka.

Where is Nagachethana PU College located?

Nagachethana PU College is located Near Raithara Santhe, Behind KIA Car Showroom, Old Town, Yelahanka, Bengaluru – 560064, Karnataka. It is easily accessible from Yelahanka New Town, Kogilu, Doddaballapura Road, and surrounding areas.
