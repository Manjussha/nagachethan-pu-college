Why Yelahanka is a Top Education Hub in North Bengaluru

According to the Department of Pre-University Education, Karnataka, Bengaluru Urban district has the highest concentration of PU colleges in the state — and Yelahanka accounts for a significant share of North Bengaluru's institutions.

  • 20+ recognised PU colleges within a 5 km radius of Yelahanka

  • Proximity to Kempegowda International Airport — making it accessible from Devanahalli, Doddaballapura, and Bagalur
  • Growing residential population driving demand for quality local education
  • Several colleges offering integrated KCET, NEET, and JEE coaching

How to Choose the Right PU College in Yelahanka

With so many options, narrowing down the best PU college requires evaluating five key factors:

  1. Pass Rate — Aim for colleges with a consistent 95%+ pass rate in Karnataka PUC board exams.
  2. Stream & Combination — Confirm your preferred stream (PCMB, PCMC, CEBA, HEBA) is offered.
  3. Entrance Exam Coaching — For Science students, integrated NEET/KCET/JEE coaching saves money and commute time.
  4. Faculty Quality — Ask about teacher qualifications and years of experience.
  5. Location — A college within 3–5 km of home significantly reduces fatigue for students.
