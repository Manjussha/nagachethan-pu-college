What is Commerce Stream in PUC?

Commerce stream in Pre-University College (PUC) is a 2-year program (1st and 2nd PUC) under the Karnataka Board that prepares students for careers in business, finance, accounting, and management. It is one of the most popular streams in Bengaluru colleges, especially for students who want to pursue CA, BCom, BBA, or MBA after Class 10.

At Nagachethana PU College, Yelahanka, Commerce stream students receive focused coaching, practical exposure to accounting and business concepts, and career guidance from experienced faculty.

CA – Chartered Accountancy

CA is the most prestigious career after PUC Commerce. Students who take CEBA in PUC and score well can register for ICAI CA Foundation directly after 2nd PUC. With dedication, CA can be completed in 4–5 years.

BCom & BCom (Hons)

BCom is the most popular undergraduate degree after PUC Commerce. Top colleges in Bengaluru like Christ University, St. Joseph's, and Bangalore University offer BCom programs. A strong PUC Commerce foundation — especially in Accountancy and Economics — is essential for BCom admission.

BBA / MBA

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) is a 3-year undergraduate management degree. After BBA or BCom, students can pursue MBA from IIMs or top private colleges. Commerce PUC students who score well in entrance exams like IPMAT can get direct BBA admission to IIM Indore and IIM Rohtak.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is CEBA in PUC Commerce?

CEBA stands for Commerce, Economics, Business Studies, and Accountancy. It is the most popular Commerce combination in Karnataka PUC, ideal for students targeting CA, BCom, or MBA careers.

What is HEBA in PUC Commerce?

HEBA stands for History, Economics, Business Studies, and Accountancy. It replaces Commerce Science with History and suits students interested in civil services, law, or humanities alongside commerce.

What careers are available after PUC Commerce?

After PUC Commerce, students can pursue CA, CMA, CS, BCom, BBA, MBA, banking and finance careers, government services, and law (LLB). Commerce stream has one of the widest career paths after Class 12.
